GOLDEN

Golden Retrievers are one of the most popular and beloved dog breeds worldwide, known for their friendly, affectionate, and loyal nature. Originating in Scotland in the late 19th century, they were initially bred to retrieve game from both land and water, thanks to their love of swimming and natural retrieving instincts. Golden Retrievers are medium to large-sized dogs, typically weighing between 55 to 75 pounds, with a dense, water-repellant coat that ranges in color from light cream to deep gold. Their coats are wavy or straight and often have characteristic "feathering" on the legs, chest, and tail. As highly intelligent dogs, Golden Retrievers are eager to please and excel in obedience training. Their natural sociability makes them wonderful companions for families, as they are gentle with children and get along well with other pets, including dogs, cats, and even smaller animals. This adaptability and intelligence also make them excellent working dogs. They often serve as guide dogs for the blind, therapy dogs, search-and-rescue dogs, and detection dogs. Their friendly and tolerant demeanor allows them to work well in public service and therapy roles, and they are frequently used in schools and hospitals for emotional support. Golden Retrievers are active and require regular physical exercise and mental stimulation..
